How I build websites

WordPress that works for your business

I use WordPress with the Blocksy theme and Elementor page builder — a combination that gives you a professional, flexible website that you can update yourself without needing a developer every time.

Every site I build is mobile-responsive from the start, loads fast, and is set up with the basics of SEO so Google can find you. I also connect Google Analytics so you can see how many people visit your site.

Once the site is live, I train you how to make basic updates yourself. If you'd prefer someone to handle ongoing maintenance, my care plans cover that too.
