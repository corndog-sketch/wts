Why Microsoft 365?

Why not just use Gmail?

Free email is fine for personal use — but for a business, it matters more than you might think. Here's why I recommend Microsoft 365 for most small businesses in Wagga.

🏢

Your own domain email

yourname@yourbusiness.com.au looks professional and builds trust. A gmail.com or hotmail.com address signals you haven't taken that step yet — and customers notice.

🔐

Enterprise-grade security

Microsoft 365 includes multi-factor authentication, advanced threat protection, and compliance tools that free accounts simply don't offer.

🤝

Built-in collaboration tools

Teams, SharePoint, and OneDrive all work together out of the box. Your team can share files, chat, and meet without juggling five different apps.

💻

Works across all your devices

Phone, laptop, desktop, tablet — everything stays in sync automatically. Start an email on your phone and finish it at your desk.

☎️

Backed by Microsoft support

As an authorised reseller I can escalate issues directly to Microsoft if needed — you're not on your own if something goes wrong.

📈

Scales with your business

Adding a new staff member? It takes minutes to add a licence and get them set up. No complicated IT infrastructure required.
