Hi, I'm Alex

Enterprise skills. Local prices. A phone that gets answered.

I'm a Bachelor of IT graduate from Charles Sturt University and a qualified L2 Systems Engineer with years of hands-on experience in enterprise environments — managing infrastructure, troubleshooting complex networks, and keeping large organisations running smoothly.

But after working in that corporate world for a while, I started noticing something frustrating: the small businesses and everyday people right here in Wagga Wagga were getting a raw deal. Dodgy cheap providers who disappeared when things went wrong, overseas call centres that couldn't tell Wagga from Wellington, and IT companies more interested in locking you into a contract than actually fixing your problem.

So I did something about it. I started Wagga Technology Solutions to bring the same quality of technical knowledge I'd applied in enterprise environments to the people and businesses who really needed it — the tradie who just wants reliable WiFi on site, the local retailer whose computer keeps freezing, the small business owner who needs a website that actually works.

When you contact me, you get me — not a call centre, not a random contractor. I show up, I explain things in plain English, I fix the problem, and I don't disappear afterwards.

My story

Why I started Wagga Technology Solutions

I'd been working in IT for years — managing systems, supporting infrastructure, solving problems for large organisations. The work was interesting, but something kept nagging at me. Every time I'd talk to a local business owner or a neighbour about their tech troubles, I'd hear the same stories: they'd called some company that never showed up, or paid a random person who made things worse, or been quoted an absurd amount for something that should have taken an hour.

The Riverina is full of hard-working small businesses — tradies, retailers, healthcare providers, farmers, cafes — and most of them are just trying to keep their technology working so they can focus on what they actually do. They don't need an IT department. They just need someone they can call who shows up, knows what they're doing, and doesn't make them feel stupid for asking.

That's the gap I decided to fill. I took my technical background, my local knowledge, and my genuine desire to help people in this community, and I built something I'm proud of. Wagga Technology Solutions is small by design — because small means personal, responsive, and accountable. You deal with me. I take responsibility. And I don't leave until the job is done right.

If you've been burned before by IT support that let you down, I get it. Give me the chance to show you what a difference it makes when your tech person actually knows your name.
