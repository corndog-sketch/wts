Sound Familiar?

Common printer problems I fix every day

🔴 "Printer showing as offline" — It's connected but Windows says it's offline. Usually a driver or network issue.

📶 "Can't find printer on WiFi" — Printer won't connect wirelessly or keeps dropping off the network.

💻 "Driver not installing" — Downloaded the driver but it won't install, or the printer isn't recognised.

🖨️ "Only one computer can print" — Need to share the printer across the whole office or household.
