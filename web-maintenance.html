Why it matters

An unmaintained WordPress site is a security risk

WordPress powers more than 40% of the web — which also makes it the most targeted platform by hackers. Outdated plugins, themes, and WordPress core are the most common entry points for attacks. I've seen plenty of hacked sites that could have been prevented with basic maintenance.

Beyond security, updates fix bugs, improve performance, and keep your site compatible with modern browsers. And if something ever goes wrong, you need a recent backup to recover from — not one from six months ago.

A care plan from me means your site gets regular attention from someone local in Wagga Wagga, not a set-and-forget script running overseas. Hosting is included in every plan — no separate bills, no confusion.
